Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Next Comes Calm is a small printable brand. We take your privacy seriously and keep things simple. Here is what we collect and why.

What we collect

We only collect your email address — and only if you choose to sign up using our email form. We do not collect any other personal information.

How we use your email

  • To send you the free sample page you signed up for
  • To let you know when new kits are available
  • Occasionally, to share a useful parent tip or guide

We do not sell your email address. We do not share it with anyone except MailerLite, the service we use to send emails.

MailerLite

Our email list is managed by MailerLite. When you sign up, your email address is stored on MailerLite's servers. You can review MailerLite's privacy policy at mailerlite.com. We use MailerLite's free tier for email delivery only.

Etsy

When you purchase a kit on Etsy, your purchase is governed by Etsy's privacy policy and terms of service. We receive only what Etsy makes available to sellers (your order details). We do not have access to your payment information.

Cookies and analytics

This website does not use cookies or any third-party analytics tracking. We do not track you across sites.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe from our emails at any time using the link at the bottom of any email we send. To request removal of your data, email us at nextcomescalm@gmail.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at nextcomescalm@gmail.com.
